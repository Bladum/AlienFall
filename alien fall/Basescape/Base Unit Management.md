# Base Unit Management

## Overview
Base Unit Management handles the housing, assignment, and development of personnel within bases. Units occupy barracks space and can be assigned to crafts or base duties. Managing units ensures operational capacity and unit progression.

## Mechanics
- **Housing**: Barracks capacity based on unit size.
- **Assignment and Activities**: Healing, training, equipping.

## Examples

Unit Activities:
- Train: Gain XP.
- Equip: Change gear.

Scenario: Unit recovers in barracks after mission.
