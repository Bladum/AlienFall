# Item Resource

## Overview
Resource Items are consumables for research/manufacturing, stored in storage, not usable by units/crafts.

## Mechanics
- **Storage**: Takes space.
- **Use**: Production.
- **Categories**: Human/alien, tiers.

## Examples

Resources:
- Alien Alloy: Construction.

Scenario: Stockpile for manufacturing.
