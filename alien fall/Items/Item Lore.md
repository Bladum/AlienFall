# Item Lore

## Overview
Lore Items are special, non-storable items used for research, with no other value.

## Mechanics
- **Storage**: No space.
- **Use**: Research only.

## Examples

Lore Items:
- Alien Artifact: Research tech.

Scenario: Discover lore item for breakthrough.
