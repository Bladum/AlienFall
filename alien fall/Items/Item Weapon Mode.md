# Item Weapon Mode

## Overview
Weapon Modes define firing configurations like burst or auto, with AP/EP costs and stat modifiers.

## Mechanics
- **Modes**: Burst, auto.
- **Costs**: AP, EP.
- **Modifiers**: Range, accuracy.

## Examples

Modes:
- Burst: +damage, -accuracy.
- Snap: Quick shot.

Scenario: Switch to burst for close combat.
