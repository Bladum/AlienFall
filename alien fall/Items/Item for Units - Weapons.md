# Item for Units - Weapons

## Overview
Unit Weapons define damage, range, effects, using energy instead of ammo, with cooldowns, AP/EP costs.

## Mechanics
- **Stats**: Damage, range.
- **Energy**: No clips.
- **Special**: Medkits, grenades.

## Examples

Weapons:
- Rifle: Standard.
- Grenade: Area damage.

Scenario: Equip sniper for long range.
