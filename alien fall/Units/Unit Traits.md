# Unit Traits

## Overview
Unit Traits are random special abilities or personalities assigned at start, providing stat boosts.

## Mechanics
- **Random**: At creation.
- **Boosts**: Stat modifiers.

## Examples

Traits:
- Lucky: +10% crit.
- Brave: +morale.

Scenario: Gain trait for bonus.
