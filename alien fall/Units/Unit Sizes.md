# Unit Sizes

## Overview
Unit Sizes categorize units as small, medium, or large, affecting battlefield space occupation. Small/medium take 1 slot; large take 2x2.

## Mechanics
- **Categories**: Small, medium, large.
- **Space**: Slot usage.
- **Tactics**: Positioning.

## Examples

Size Effects:
- Small: 1x1, agile.
- Large: 2x2, tanky.

Scenario: Large mech blocks path.
