# Research Entry

## Overview
Individual research topics with description, cost, image, requiring prerequisites.

## Mechanics
- **Requirements**: Research, facilities.
- **Blocks**: Other research.
- **Start**: Assign people.

## Examples

Entry:
- Topic: Psionics.
- Cost: 1000 credits.

Scenario: Unlock psionic tech.
