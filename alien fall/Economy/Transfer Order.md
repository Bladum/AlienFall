# Transfer Order

## Overview
Transfer Orders handle delivery of goods to bases, used for purchases and manufacturing.

## Mechanics
- **Delivery**: N days.
- **Uses**: Market, inter-base, production.

## Examples

Order:
- Goods: Rifles.
- Destination: Base 2.
- Time: 5 days.

Scenario: Transfer troops between bases.
