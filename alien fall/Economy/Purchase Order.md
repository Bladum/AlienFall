# Purchase Order

## Overview
Players create orders by selecting entries, base, quantity, generating transfer orders.

## Mechanics
- **Grouping**: By delivery.
- **Impacts**: Monthly limits, karma.
- **Transfers**: Automatic.

## Examples

Order:
- Item: Rifle.
- Quantity: 10.
- Base: HQ.

Scenario: Order delivered via transfer.
