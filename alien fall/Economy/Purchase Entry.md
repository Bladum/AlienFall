# Purchase Entry

## Overview
Individual marketplace offers for buying goods, with price, supplier, availability, delivery.

## Mechanics
- **Details**: Price, supplier.
- **Requirements**: Tech, contact.

## Examples

Entry:
- Item: Rifle.
- Price: 500 credits.
- Delivery: 3 days.

Scenario: Buy rifle for squad.
