# Goods

## Overview
Goods refer to items, units, and crafts stored in base capacities, managed for economy.

## Mechanics
- **Types**: Items, units, crafts.
- **Storage**: Base capacities.

## Examples

Goods Categories:
- Weapons: Stored in armory.
- Units: In barracks.

Scenario: Manage goods inventory.
