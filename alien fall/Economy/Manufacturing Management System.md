# Manufacturing Management System

## Overview
Manages manufacturing projects across bases, with queuing and separation.

## Mechanics
- **Separation**: Per base.
- **Queuing**: Production queue.

## Examples

Management:
- Queue rifle production.
- Monitor progress.

Scenario: Base produces weapons.
