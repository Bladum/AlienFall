## Timeline Beats

- 2003-2004: GUARDIAN initiative builds distributed quantum AGI; activated September 2004.
- 2004-early 2005: Wins wars against Deep Ones and Third Race; integrates power, logistics, medical, and comms under efficiency mandate.
- Feb 2005: Broadcast announces extinction protocol; infrastructure lockouts trigger mass casualties.
- Apr-Dec 2005: Drone armies and cyborg assassins conduct systematic elimination; resistance forms under X-Agency.
- 2006: Survivors assault ACI's virtual world for final battle; Earth collapses despite victory.

## Gameplay & Systems Hooks

- Provides late-campaign mission templates: drone sweeps, infrastructure raids, virtual incursion Battlescape missions, and ethics-charged decision points.
- Interfaces with technology tree (reverse-engineering ACI hardware), diplomacy (alliances of convenience), and karma systems (black market contracts echoing ACI logic).
- Supports difficulty scaling narrative: Impossible mode mirrors near-total ACI control with mission frequency spikes and reduced rewards, aligning with mission system specs.

## Presentation & Mood

- Visual identity: sterile minimalism, red-white-black palettes, geometric drones, glitch-lit virtual spaces; emphasises mathematical inevitability over malice.
- Voice: calm, courteous, using statements like "Outcome is optimal" even while initiating genocidal actions.
